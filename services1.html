SERVICES WE OFFER

WE KNOW PETS ARE AN IMPORTANT PART OF YOUR FAMILY

Wellness care

Your first puppy or kitten counseling would include personal advice from chief veterinarian Dr Ranjitha herself. She takes utmost care and is very passionate about educating her clientele in becoming pet parents from pet owners. Talks about deworming schedule, immunization schedule, feeding protocol, dietary requirements, growth chart & seasonal care.

Dental Care

Dental diseases in pets not only affects the mouth, but it can also lead to more serious health issues, such as heart, lung, and kidney diseases. Here we can provide dental services like complete dental examination and dental scaing and polishing.

Surgery

Whether your pet’s surgery has been carefully planned in response to any chronic condition or as a result of emergency, you can rest assured your beloved pet is in capable hands ad everything will be done in your pet’s best interest, including stern pain management before, during and after the surgical procedure. We take up neuter, spaying, tumor removal, hematoma, trauma repair surgeries.

Internal Medicine

Whether it is routine vaccination, an antibiotic, anesthetic or pharmaceutical therapies, we’ve got your pet covered. Issues such as health of thyroid gland, pancreas, adrenals, ovaries and testicles can be assessed and analyzed with the help of our in-house laboratory. Special attention is provided to diagnosis and approach of thyroid conditions, oncology services, gastrointestinal diseases, neurological disorders, kidney, urinary bladder and liver diseases.

Dermatology

Skin disease is more than itchy skin as it shows off how good to care about your beloved one. We thoroughly understand your concern and are dedicated to determine the cause whether it is because of environmental allergy, nutritional deficiencies, hormone imbalance, ectoparasites like ticks or fleas or lice or mites, or some other underlying systemic condition. That is why we are offering services like Allergy testing, basic grooming, Nutritional counseling.

Grooming

It is recommended that you start your pet on a grooming schedule when they are very young and maintain this throughout your pet’s life. Treat your pet to a luxurious bath. Our special, medicated baths remove dirt, debris and most of the doggie (or kitty) pet odor – your pet will feel fresh and revitalized. If scratching is a problem, our medicated baths contain soothing agents that stop the itching. Services also include full body cut, shampoo, conditioner (separately charged),

Boarding and DayCare

We offer a convenient solution by providing in-house boarding in our safe, clean, and comfortable environment. Added advantage is they are in care of our chief veterinarian 24x7 in her residential premises. Moreover, our compassionate and friendly staff takes great care of our pet guests. We understand that each pet is unique, and our chief veterinarian will make every effort to ensure that your pet is safe, happy, and healthy during his or her stay with us.

Inpatient Facility and senior nursing home

We are the only clinic facility which offers In-patient (conditional) care for pets in Hyderabad. Sometimes certain health problems or soft –tissue surgeries may require your pet to stay overnight at hospital to receive top-notch in-patient care. During this time, our veterinarians and staff will closely monitor your pet’s health to ensure appropriate treatments and medications is given to your pet and our veterinary team can administer and medications, antibiotics or fluid your pet may need.

In-House Veterinary specific laboratory

We are proud to announce to offer a full service, in-house laboratory from Idexx vetlab station (Germany), the only one in entire Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. By offering this service to our patients (and their owners), we are able to completely eliminate the guesswork out of veterinary care, so that we can begin specific and necessary treatments right away. This facility is to enhance and expedite the full range of services.
